Arroz Doce

A dessert that's easy to make, and even easier to eat!

Ingredients

  • 5 Eggs
  • 385g of condensed milk
  • 50g of sliced ​​almonds
  • Chocolate shavings as needed

Preparation time

2:30 Minutes

Preparation method

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the whites and set the whites aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine the egg yolks and the cooked condensed milk. Beat well with an electric mixer until you obtain a smooth mixture and set aside.
  3. In another bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form. Gently fold them into the previous mixture using a spatula.
  4. Place the camel's milk pudding in glass cups and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  5. Toast the sliced ​​almonds in a non-stick frying pan until golden brown. Set aside.
  6. Remove the camel's milk pudding from the refrigerator just before serving. Garnish with toasted sliced ​​almonds and chocolate shavings.
