Arroz Doce

A typical Portuguese sweet, steeped in tradition and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 and a half cups of rice
  • 2 and a half cups of water
  • 5 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of heavy cream
  • Sugar to taste
  • Ground or stick cinnamon

Preparation time

1:30 horas

Preparation method

  1. Add the water, rice, and cinnamon and let it boil until the water is absorbed.
  2. Next, add the milk, vanilla, sugar, and condensed milk, stirring constantly.
  3. Let it boil for 15 minutes, then add the cream and stir.
  4. Boil for another 5 minutes and it's ready.
  5. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  6. It can be served hot or cold, according to preference.
