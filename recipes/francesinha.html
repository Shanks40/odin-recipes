Francesinha

A typical dish from Porto, it is advisable to always serve it with homemade fries.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 330ml of beer
  • 100g of chopped tomato
  • 30g of butter
  • 15ml of Perrins or Worcestershire sauce
  • 20ml of vegetable broth
  • 10g of fine cornmeal
  • 3 slices of white bread
  • 2 pork loin fillets
  • 1 slice of cooked ham
  • 2 or 3 sausages
  • 8 slices of cheese for sandwiches (melting cheese)

Preparation time

2:30 horas

Preparation method

  1. Start by sauteing the chopped onion and garlic in olive oil. When golden brown, add the beer and then the remaining ingredients, except for the cornstarch. Let it simmer for about 15 minutes.
  2. Separately, dissolve the cornstarch in a little water. Gradually add it to the sauce until it reaches your desired consistency. Remove the pan from the heat, take out the bay leaf, and blend everything with an immersion blender. Return to the heat and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

    2. A slice of ham

    5. A slice of cheese
    6. Another slice of bread on top
    7. Now you should put it in the toaster to toast the bread a little.
    8. Cover with 5 slices of cheese.
    9. Place the egg
    10. Place in a toaster (or oven) to melt the cheese.
